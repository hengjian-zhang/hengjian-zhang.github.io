Hello there! I am a PhD candidate in Financial Mathematics at the Department of Mathematics, London School of Economics and Political Science, advised by Professor Mihail Zervos.

My research interests lie at stochastic control, reinforcement learning and mathematical finance. So far, I have worked on singular stochastic control problems in which the parameters of SDE are known  I am now especially interested in using reinforcement learning methods to solve singular stochastic control problems with unknown parameters.

If you are also interested in reinforcement learning and stochastic control, I’m very happy to discuss and collaborate, please do not hesitate to reach out.
